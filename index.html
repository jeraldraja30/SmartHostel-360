About Us

Our Hostels

Welcome to University Hostels, providing comfortable and secure accommodation for students since 2005. Our hostels are designed to create a home away from home for students pursuing their academic dreams.

Our History

Founded in 2005, we have been serving students for over 18 years. We started with just one building and have expanded to multiple locations across the city.

Our Vision

To create a safe, comfortable, and conducive living environment that supports academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Mission

To provide quality accommodation with modern amenities at affordable prices, ensuring students can focus on their studies without worries.

Our Team

Our dedicated team works around the clock to ensure the safety, comfort, and well-being of all our residents.

Wardens

Experienced wardens oversee each hostel block, ensuring discipline and addressing student concerns promptly.

Support Staff

Our support staff including cleaners, maintenance personnel, and security guards work diligently to maintain our facilities.

Administration

The administrative team manages bookings, payments, and day-to-day operations efficiently.

Achievements

Over the years, we have received numerous accolades for our services and facilities.

  • Best Student Hostel Award 2022
  • Green Campus Initiative Award 2021
  • Safety & Security Excellence Award 2020
  • Student Satisfaction Award 2019
